3D Mapping and Survey

Whether your business is Construction, Agriculture, Mining or any other business that manages or works on terrain, 3D models and maps can aid in the decision making process. Having a accurate digital map of a work site can be beneficial in some of the following ways:

Logistics and Route Planning

Project Planning and Progress Monitoring

Work Site Security and Inspection

Roof Modeling, Inspection, Geometry Reports

Using our aerial platforms we capture Hi-Def images and use those images to produce 3D models and elevation maps. The results are viewable in a compatible web browser on a computer, tablet, or smartphone. Printed format is also available.

Agricultural Analysis

Using our aerial platforms we capture Hi-Def images and use those images to produce high quality maps and reports informing you of the status of your crops. These reports can be valuable resource for things such as:

Crop Variability Detection

Crop Loss Insurance Reporting

Plant health analysis using NDVI and VARI Image Technologies

Stand Count and Population Analysis

For best results a few flights should be flown over the course of a few weeks. The change over time can yield even more incites. The reports generated are viewable in a compatible web browser on a computer, tablet, or smartphone. Printed format is also available.
